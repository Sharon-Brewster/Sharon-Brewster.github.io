About Me

Creating Inclusive Futures

Hello! I'm Sharon Brewster, a passionate Special Educator and Software Engineer dedicated to making a meaningful difference in the lives of children with special needs. My unique combination of technical expertise and educational knowledge allows me to create innovative solutions for inclusive learning environments.

Currently serving as a Special Education Teacher at Devdan Foundation Learning Centre in Mysore, I work with children experiencing various developmental and learning challenges, from Autism Spectrum Disorders to Specific Learning Disabilities.

My background in technology - including an MCA degree, banking IT operations experience, and ethical hacking certification - enables me to leverage cutting-edge tools and platforms to enhance special education delivery.

My Mission: To bridge the gap between technology and special education, creating accessible, effective, and compassionate learning experiences for every child, regardless of their abilities.
